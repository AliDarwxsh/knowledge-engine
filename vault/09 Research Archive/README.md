# 09 Research Archive

> Research is a project, not a folder. Every research effort has a deadline and an output.

---

## Purpose

This folder holds **research projects** — temporary, structured investigations with a defined question, a deadline, and an output. Research archives differ from [[04 Knowledge Library|04 Knowledge Library]] in three ways:

| Property | Research Archive | Knowledge Library |
|----------|------------------|------------------|
| Time-bounded | Yes — has a start and end | No — accumulates indefinitely |
| Question-driven | Yes — has a central question | No — organized by domain |
| Output-driven | Yes — produces a deliverable | No — references for retrieval |

---

## What Lives Here

| Note Type | Purpose | Filename |
|-----------|---------|----------|
| Research project | A structured investigation | `research-topic.md` |
| Research log | Running log of findings during a project | `log-topic.md` |
| Research report | Final deliverable | `report-topic.md` |

---

## Anatomy of a Research Project

Every research project note contains:

1. **Central question** — the one question this research answers
2. **Hypothesis** — the operator's prior belief
3. **Scope** — what is in, what is out
4. **Sources** — primary sources via [[wikilinks]] to [[sources/README|sources]]
5. **Method** — how the research will proceed
6. **Output** — what deliverable will be produced
7. **Deadline** — when the research must conclude

> [!tip] **A research project without a hypothesis is a reading list.** State the prior.

---

## When to Open

- When a new investigation begins (capture first in [[00 Inbox]], promote here)
- When preparing a major decision that requires evidence
- When building a deliverable that depends on external sources
- During a quarterly research review

---

## How to Use

1. **Promote from [[00 Inbox|00 Inbox]]** when the question is defined.
2. **Log as you go.** A research log per project captures findings in real time.
3. **Distill into [[04 Knowledge Library|04 Knowledge Library]]** when claims graduate to durable knowledge.
4. **Close with a report.** Every research project ends with a deliverable in `report-*.md`.
5. **Archive after closure.** Closed research projects move to `archive/research/`.

---

## Distillation Flow

```
sources/ (raw, immutable)
   ↓
research archive (time-bounded investigation)
   ↓
knowledge library (durable domain notes)
   ↓
principles (behavioral rules derived from knowledge)
```

> [!warning] **Sources are immutable.** Once material is in [[sources/README|sources]], do not edit it. Distill into new notes instead.
