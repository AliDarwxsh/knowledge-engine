# 10 Ideas & Opportunities

> An idea without a trigger is a daydream. An idea with a trigger is a queued project.

---

## Purpose

This folder holds the operator's **ideas and opportunities** — possibilities that have surfaced but are not yet committed to. The folder is a structured holding pen: ideas move up to [[03 Projects|03 Projects]] when a triggering condition is met, or down to `archive/` when they lose relevance.

| Layer | Holds |
|-------|-------|
| [[10 Ideas & Opportunities\|10 Ideas & Opportunities]] | Pre-project, pre-decision possibilities |
| [[03 Projects\|03 Projects]] | Committed, resourced work |
| [[06 Decision Archive\|06 Decision Archive]] | Decisions made, including decisions to defer |

---

## What Lives Here

| Note Type | Purpose | Filename |
|-----------|---------|----------|
| Idea | A single concept with potential | `idea-name.md` |
| Opportunity | A time-bound opening | `opportunity-name.md` |
| Idea cluster | A group of related ideas | `cluster-name.md` |

---

## Anatomy of an Idea Note

Every idea note answers four questions:

1. **What is the idea?** — one paragraph
2. **Why does it matter?** — the upside
3. **What would it cost to test?** — minimum viable version
4. **What is the trigger?** — the condition under which this becomes a [[03 Projects|project]]

> [!tip] **The trigger field is non-negotiable.** An idea without a trigger is a wish.

---

## Lifecycle

```
00 Inbox (capture)
   ↓
10 Ideas & Opportunities (evaluate)
   ↓ (trigger met)
03 Projects (commit)
   ↓ (closed)
06 Decision Archive (decision log)
```

A dormant idea either moves to `archive/ideas/` or stays with an updated `last_reviewed` date.

---

## When to Open

- During weekly review to triage new ideas
- During quarterly review to prune dormant ones
- When a known trigger fires — promote to [[03 Projects|03 Projects]]
- When seeking creative input for synthesis work

---

## How to Use

1. **Capture freely.** Most ideas arrive in [[00 Inbox|00 Inbox]] first; promote here after a sentence or two of evaluation.
2. **Set a trigger.** Without one, an idea rots.
3. **Set a `last_reviewed` date.** Quarterly review re-evaluates each idea.
4. **Connect ideas.** Use [[wikilinks]] to link ideas to principles, decisions, or other ideas.
5. **Promote decisively.** When a trigger fires, the idea becomes a project within seven days or it is archived.
