---

## EOD Reflection Prompts

The evening reflection is the heart of the daily note. The five prompts are non-negotiable:

1. **What went well?** — surface wins that would otherwise fade.
2. **What drained me?** — surface energy drains; recurring patterns become principles.
3. **What did I learn?** — surface new knowledge; promotes to [[04 Knowledge Library]].
4. **What would I do differently?** — surface process improvements; promotes to [[07 Systems & SOPs]].
5. **One thing I am grateful for.** — anchor in what is working.

> [!tip] **Time budget:** 5–7 minutes. The reflection should feel like closure, not homework.

---

## When to Use

- Every evening, during or after the [[workflows/daily-rhythm|evening pass]].
- Never in advance — daily notes are dated, not predicted.
- Never edited after the date passes — corrections go in the next day's note.
