# Daily Rhythm — The Five-Minute Evening Pass

> Five minutes per evening. The vault stays clean. The mind stays uncluttered.

---

## Purpose

The Daily Rhythm is the **single non-negotiable SOP** in the vault. It runs every evening, takes five minutes, and consists of one operation: clear the [[00 Inbox|inbox]].

Without this ritual, the inbox accumulates. Accumulated inboxes become write-only folders. Write-only folders become the operator's graveyard of good intentions.

> [!warning] **If you skip the evening pass more than twice in a week, the system has already failed.** Re-commit the next day; do not let the streak rebuild from zero.

---

## Preconditions

- The vault is open in Obsidian.
- The operator has five uninterrupted minutes.
- The inbox has at least one item or the operator is checking for zero.

---

## Time Budget

| Step | Duration |
|------|----------|
| Open inbox | 30 seconds |
| Triage each item | 30–60 seconds |
| Close inbox | 30 seconds |
| **Total** | **5 minutes** |

If a single item takes longer than 60 seconds, it gets parked in [[10 Ideas & Opportunities|10 Ideas]] and triaged during the weekly review.

---

## The Procedure

### Step 1 — Open the Inbox (30s)
Open [[00 Inbox|00 Inbox]]. Scan the items in capture order. Do not read; glance.

### Step 2 — Apply the Four-Path Tree (4 minutes)
For each item, ask one question and pick exactly one path:

| Path | When | Destination |
|------|------|-------------|
| **DO** | Will be acted on within 7 days | Promote to [[03 Projects\|03 Projects]] as a task with a `next_action` |
| **KEEP** | Reference value, no action | Move to [[04 Knowledge Library\|04 Knowledge Library]], [[05 Framework Library\|05 Framework Library]], [[10 Ideas & Opportunities\|10 Ideas]], or [[12 Relationship Maps\|12 Relationship Maps]] |
| **FILE** | Personal, emotional, or private | Move to [[01 Identity\|01 Identity]] or [[11 Timeline\|11 Timeline]] |
| **DELETE** | No durable value | Move to `archive/trash/` or delete |

Full decision tree with examples: [[workflows/inbox-triage\|workflows/inbox-triage.md]].

### Step 3 — Close the Inbox (30s)
- Confirm inbox count is zero.
- Log a one-line note in the day's [[templates/daily-note|daily note]]: "Evening pass cleared N items."
- Close Obsidian or move on.

---

## Common Failure Modes

| Failure | Recovery |
|---------|----------|
| Inbox has 30+ items | Triage top 10 only; flag rest for weekly review |
| Item resists categorization | Park in [[10 Ideas & Opportunities\|10 Ideas]] with `unclear` tag |
| Triage exceeds 5 minutes | Stop after 5 minutes; remainder carries to tomorrow |
| Inbox empty but mind is full | Run a 60-second voice memo into inbox, then triage |
| Skipped a day | Do not double-up the next day; do the regular pass |

---

## Variations

### Travel Day
If the operator is traveling, the evening pass becomes:
- Voice memos only into [[00 Inbox|inbox]].
- Triage the next morning, not the evening.
- Time budget extends to 10 minutes.

### High-Volume Day
If the inbox has 20+ items:
- Triage top 10 in the five-minute window.
- Schedule a 15-minute "deep triage" block the next morning for the rest.

### Zero-Item Day
If the inbox is empty:
- Confirm zero.
- Note in daily note: "Inbox zero. Streak: N days."
- Done in 60 seconds.
